🤸 Mallakhamb

State sportMallakhamb was made MP state game in 2013.
Women typeRope Mallakhamb is especially performed by women players.
AcademyMallakhamb Academy: Ujjain. Establishment: 2018.
CentresMP mein 14 Mallakhamb centres bataye gaye.
AwardPrabhash Joshi Award is linked with Mallakhamb.
Guru / PioneerAcharya Vallabh Bhatt Deodhar.
Mal + Khamb = wrestler body + pole. Pole/fixed mostly men; rope wali image women ke saath lock karo.
Material: Fixed/pole Mallakhamb usually teak/sagwan or sheesham wood se banta hai. Niche wide, upar narrow.
Quick Quiz
Rope Mallakhamb.
Ujjain.
Acharya Vallabh Bhatt Deodhar.

🪶 Tribes High-Yield

⭐ Unit 10 Strategy: Lecturer ne bola every class mein tribal questions honge. PVTG, dance, location, festivals aur PYQ facts par focus.
Tribe / TopicKey FactsMnemonic
BhariaPVTG. Meaning: lack of knowledge, yani external duniya ka knowledge kam. Patal Kot, Tamia tehsil, Chhindwara; some population in Jabalpur. Patal Kot mein around 90% Bharia population. Valley horse-shoe shaped.Bharia = bahari duniya ka knowledge kam.
Bharia womenWhite bead necklace, bunches, brass/iron bangles pasand karti hain.White beads = Patal Kot visual.
KorkuLiteral meaning: group of humans. Main area: Betul. Bandori/Korku query aaye to Pachmarhi, Narmadapuram recall.Korku = core group of people.
Korku cultureDance: Chhatkora. Song: Relo. Crops: bajra, kodo, kutki, maize. Kharif ke baad labour and minor forest produce.Chhatkora mein "kor"; railo song rail mein yaad.
HalwaMP mein Balaghat area se linked.Halwa bachchon ko pasand: Bala-ghat.
Kundar / KunderKherwar se related. Chhatarpur, Tikamgarh, Panna. Khair/katha collection link.Kherwar = khair tree.
KolDahka is primitive dance. Areas: Rewa, Sidhi, Satna, Shahdol.Coal dahan karta hai, Dahka = Kol.
Kol MahakumbhShabari Mata Jayanti occasion par Satna mein 2023 reference.Satna + Shabari + Kol.
Tribal Pride DayBirsa Munda Jayanti: 15 November. Decision/announcement as annual day: 2021, as per lecture answer.
PESA ActAct year: 1996. MP ke 89 tribal development blocks mein 15 Nov 2022 ko लागू. Launch context: Shahdol, Droupadi Murmu present.
Vimukt Jati DiwasMPPSC PYQ: 31 August.
Badal BhoiTribal freedom fighter. Badal Bhoi Tribal Museum: Chhindwara, 1954.
