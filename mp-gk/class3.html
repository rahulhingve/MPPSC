🎯 Madhya Pradesh ka Gathan & Partition

Video transcript se banaya gaya Hinglish revision page: 1947 ke baad commissions, 1956 me MP ka formation, capital issue, divisions, district timeline aur 2000 me Chhattisgarh partition.

🧭 Class Recap & Context

Teacher ne previous lecture ka recap diya aur current lecture ko “Formation and Partition of Madhya Pradesh” bola.

Last Class me kya hua?

  • India aur Madhya Pradesh ko independence ke baad dekha gaya tha.
  • Administrative units: Part A, Part B, Part C aur Part D ka context.
  • MP ke liye Part A, B, C aur Bhopal State discuss hua tha.
  • Is lecture me wahin se आगे formation ka part start hota hai.

Core Issue

  • Independence ke baad states ko language basis par banane ki demand badhi.
  • South India me Tamil, Telugu, Kannada language groups se movement ki “चिंगारी” उठी.
  • Teacher ne bola: “partition” se zyada सही word yahan “formation” hai.

Quick Recall

Q. Lecture ka main topic kya hai?
Madhya Pradesh ka gathan, formation aur partition.
Q. Last class me MP ke kaunse parts dekhe the?
Part A, Part B, Part C aur Bhopal State.

🔥 Potti Sriramulu & Andhra Pradesh

Dhar aur JVP ke rejection ke baad Telugu-speaking logon ka movement तेज ho gaya.

Movement

  • Madras State ke Telugu-speaking people ne separate state demand ki.
  • Leadership: Potti Sriramulu.
  • Demand: Telugu speakers ke liye अलग राज्य.

Turning Point

  • 15 December 1952: Potti Sriramulu ki death.
  • Cause: 58 days ki hunger strike / आमरण अनशन.
  • Death ke baad movement ki intensity बहुत बढ़ गई.

🔥 Exam Hot

1 October 1953 ko independent India ka first linguistic-basis state Andhra Pradesh formed hua. Teacher ne bola: “एक बार exam me पूछा जा चुका है.”

Dhar/JVP reject ↓ Telugu movement in Madras ↓ Potti Sriramulu death: 15 Dec 1952 ↓ Nehru announces Andhra Pradesh ↓ 1 Oct 1953: First linguistic state

Quick Recall

Q. Potti Sriramulu kis movement se जुड़े थे?
Telugu-speaking people ke separate Andhra Pradesh movement se.
Q. Andhra Pradesh kab bana?
1 October 1953.

🏛️ Bhopal Capital ka Selection

Capital ke liye discussion me Jabalpur recommendation aur Bhopal support dono aaye.

Jabalpur Recommendation

  • Fazal Ali ne Jabalpur ko capital recommend kiya tha.
  • Baad me Jabalpur ko judicial capital role mila.
  • High Court Jabalpur me रखा गया.

Bhopal kyun?

  • Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma ne Bhopal ko capital banane ka पक्ष रखा.
  • Bhopal already developed urban area tha.
  • Nawab/Begum era ke large buildings available the, administrative use ke liye.

Old Vidhan Sabha building ke taur par Minto Hall ka use 1956 se 1996 tak, lagbhag 40 saal, hua.

Quick Recall

Jabalpur.
Q. Bhopal ke पक्ष me teacher ne kaunse leader ka naam बताया?
Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma.
