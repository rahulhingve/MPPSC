🧭 4-Part Framework

⭐ Exam Hotspot: MP formation ko 4 phases mein yaad rakho: pre-1947, 1947-1956, 1956-2000, aur 1 November 2000 ke baad.

1. 1947 se pehle

British rule ke time MP mein direct aur indirect control dono the.

2. 1947 se 1956

Princely states ko Part A, B, C mein organize kiya gaya.

3. 1956 se 2000

1956 ke formation ke baad unified Madhya Pradesh ka phase.

4. 1 November 2000 se aaj tak

Chhattisgarh separation ke baad present Madhya Pradesh ka phase.

Memory Hook

“P-F-D-P”: Pehle British, Formation base, Division se pehle, Present MP.

Quick Quiz

4 phases.
Partition ke baad present Madhya Pradesh.

📜 Pre-1947: British Control ka Base

⭐ Exam Hotspot: MP mein British ka first major entry point 1818 Third Anglo-Maratha War ke baad aaya.
1740

Gondwana par Maratha control

Raghuji I ne Gondwana par attack kiya. Gond rulers ko defeat karke Maratha control establish hua.

1818

Third Anglo-Maratha War

Marathas haare. Treaty ke baad British ne Gondwana/Mahakoshal, Sagar aur Narmada region par control liya.

1853

Doctrine of Lapse

Raghoji III ke heir na hone par Nagpur-Vidarbha British control mein gaya. ASR mein “1833” sunai deta hai; exam date 1853 rakho.

1854

Central India Agency

Gwalior, Indore, Bundelkhand, Baghelkhand jaise indirect control areas ke liye CIA bani. Headquarters Indore aur first resident R. Hamilton bataya gaya.

1857

Revolt ka pressure

Gondwana, Mahakoshal, Nagpur, Narmada aur Sagar mein revolt hua. British direct control ko strong karne lage.

1861

Central Provinces / Madhya Prant

2 November 1861 ko Commissioner area bana. Headquarters Nagpur tha.

1903

Berar lease

Hyderabad ke Nizam Mahbub Ali se Berar region 25 lakh annual income par British administration ko lease hua.

1936

Central Provinces and Berar

24 October 1936 ko Berar fully merge hoke Central Provinces and Berar naam strong hua.

Direct Control

British officer directly baitha tha. Central Provinces and Berar iska main example hai.

Indirect Control

Local ruler naam ka ruler tha, par decision British line par chalta tha.

Common Trap

French aur Portuguese areas India mein the, par MP ke formation chapter mein focus British direct/indirect control par hai.

Mnemonic

1740 Maratha aaye, 1818 British aaye, 1853 Nagpur gaya, 1936 Berar jud gaya.

Quick Quiz

1818.
Indirect control.
Nagpur.

🗂️ 1947 ke Baad: A-B-C System

⭐ Exam Hotspot: MP ke paas Part A, Part B, Part C the. Part D island territories jaise Andaman-Nicobar ke liye tha.
Part Control Type MP Example Exam Line
Part A British direct control wali badi province Central Provinces and Berar Nagpur capital; 15 princely states.
Part B British indirect control wali badi states Madhya Bharat / Malwa Union Gwalior aur Indore core regions.
Part C British indirect control wali chhoti states Vindhya Pradesh + Bhopal 38 states + separate Bhopal count.

Total Count

Part A 15 + Part B 25 + Part C 38 = 78. Bhopal add karo: 79.

Trap

Bhopal ko count mein alag yaad rakho. 78 ke baad plus Bhopal se 79 banta hai.

Quick Quiz

79.
Part D island/centrally administered territories ke liye tha.

🅰️ Part A: Central Provinces and Berar

⭐ Exam Hotspot: Part A ka capital Nagpur, CM Ravi Shankar Shukla, 22 districts, 5 divisions aur 15 princely states.

Formation

1 January 1948 ko Part A ke roop mein setup bataya gaya.

Capital

Nagpur. Same headquarters continuity yaad rakho.

First Governor

Lecture note: E. Raghavendra Rao. Revision mein “lecture fact” ke roop mein mark karo.

First CM

Pandit Ravi Shankar Shukla. Later Madhya Pradesh ke first CM bhi bane.

Administrative Count

22 districts aur 5 divisions.

Regions

Mahakoshal, Sagar/Narmada, Nagpur-Vidarbha, Chhattisgarh, Bastar, Berar.

🅲 Part C: Vindhya Pradesh

⭐ Exam Hotspot: Part C ka capital Rewa, Raj Pramukh Martand Singh, 38 princely states aur 60-member assembly.

Formation

12 March 1948 ko B. P. Menon ke role se setup bataya gaya.

Inauguration

4 April 1948 ko N. V. Gadgil ne inauguration kiya.

Name Shift

1 January 1950 se Vindhya Pradesh ko Part C mein include karke area ka naam Vindhya Pradesh hua.

Capital

Rewa. Sub-capital: Nowgong, Chhatarpur.

Leadership

Raj Pramukh: Martand Singh. Up-Raj Pramukh: Yadvendra Singh of Panna.

Government

First CM: Shambhunath Shukla. First Speaker: Shri Shivanand.

Counts

38 princely states. Assembly: 60 members.

🏛️ Bhopal State Integration

⭐ Exam Hotspot: Bhopal ne 30 April 1949 ko merger agreement sign kiya aur 1 June 1949 ko Indian Union mein merge hua.
1818

British rule link

Bhopal princely state British rule ke under 1818 se linked bataya gaya.

1947

Independent rehne ki koshish

Nawab Hamidullah Khan Bhopal ko independent rakhna chahte the.

1949

Boras massacre

14 January 1949, Boras village, Raisen. Isko lecture mein “Bhopal ka Jallianwala Bagh” bola gaya.

1949

Merger

30 April 1949 ko signature. 1 June 1949 ko Bhopal Indian Union mein merge hua.

Praja Mandal Movement

Bhopal ko Indian Union mein merge karane ke liye movement chala.

Key Leader

Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma. Nawab ne unko jail bheja.

First CM

Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma. 1952 election ke baad 1956 tak CM bataye gaye.

Chief Commissioner

N. B. Banerjee.

Assembly

30-member assembly. Speaker: Sultan Mohammad.

Deputy Speaker

Laxmi Narayan Agrawal.

Memory Hook

“Boras pressure, Bhopal merger”: 14 Jan pressure, 30 Apr sign, 1 Jun merge.

Quick Quiz

30 April 1949.
1 June 1949.
Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma.
