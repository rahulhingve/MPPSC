⛰️ पर्वत किसे कहते हैं? Mountain ka basic concept

🔎 Definition in Hinglish

  • पर्वत / Mountain ऐसी स्पष्ट उच्च भूमि hai jo surrounding land se upar uthi hoti hai.
  • Isme एक या एक से अधिक शिखर / peaks ho sakte hain.
  • Upar ka भाग / pinnacle संकुचित या सकरा hota hai.
  • Iska ढाल / slope तीव्र / intense hota hai.
  • Prelims me definition likhne ko nahi aayegi, par concept dimaag me clear rehna chahiye.

🧱 पठार vs पर्वत

Q. Plateau aur mountain me main visual difference?
Plateau ka upper part flat hota hai; mountain ka peak narrow aur slope तीव्र hota hai.

🏔️ Vindhyachal Mountain Range — पुरानी और अवशिष्ट पर्वत श्रृंखला

📍 Extent / विस्तार

  • Narmada valley aur Son valley ki northern boundary ke around samjhai gayi.
  • Western MP se lekar east me Bihar/Jharkhand tak ka reference diya gaya.
  • Parasnath hills tak iska touch/extension bataya gaya.
  • Rajasthan-Gujarat side par Aravalli range ka relation explain kiya gaya.
  • Teacher ne warn kiya: present me ye continuous nahi hai; kahin-kahin dikhta hai aur kahin dhans gaya / missing jaisa hai.

🪨 Type / प्रकार

  • प्राचीन पर्वत श्रृंखला — old mountain range.
  • अवशिष्ट पर्वत / Residual Mountain — chattanon ke टूटने / अपर्दन se bana-bacha hua form.
  • Aravalli ko duniya ki sabse purani mountain range bataya; Vindhyachal ko usse related old region ke roop me explain kiya.
  • Old mountains ka form time ke saath 낮ा/झुका hua dikh sakta hai; teacher ne “वृद्धावस्था” analogy di.
🔬 Teacher's analogy: purana mountain kyon toot/dhans sakta hai?

Teacher ne plate movement, earthquake aur samay ke saath mountain ke old hone ka idea diya. Jitna purana mountain, utna uska peak comparatively lower/eroded ho sakta hai. Example me Himalaya ko newer folded mountain aur Aravalli ko old range ke roop me contrast kiya.

Unhone relatable line di: “समय के साथ दूरियां बढ़ जाती हैं” — Aravalli aur Vindhyachal pehle touch/connected samjhe ja sakte the, ab beech me gap/distance aa gayi.

🧭 Kaimur Range — Eastern Vindhyachal

📌 Location + length clues

  • Eastern part ko Kaimur Range / कैमूर श्रेणी bola gaya.
  • MP context me Singrauli side se start samjhaya.
  • Katni/Damoh ke paas tak ka eastern stretch bataya.
  • Overall range ka extension Jharkhand/Parasnath hills tak reference me aaya.
  • Total length teacher ne approx 1066–1086 km ke range me batayi.

⛩️ Trikuta + Sharda Mata

  • Trikuta hill / त्रिकुटा पहाड़ी Maihar ke paas batayi.
  • Isi par Sharda Mata Temple ka reference diya gaya.
  • Exam me Sharda Mata / Trikuta hill puchhe to Vindhyachal/Kaimur connection yaad rakho.
Watershed / जल-द्विभाजक: Kaimur ko Yamuna aur Son river ke beech watershed ka kaam karne wala bataya. Ken aur Betwa jaise rivers Yamuna me milti hain, par Son Ganga se milti hai — beech me Kaimur/Vindhyachal barrier samjha.

🌞 “Vindhya” naam ka explanation

  • Teacher ne Sanskrit word वेद/वैध se “बाधा डालना” wali explanation di.
  • Story: Vindhyachal itna uncha tha ki surya ki kirnon me बाधा daalta tha.
  • Agastya Rishi ne isko roka/samjhaya — mythological reference.
  • MP me Singrauli side se surya ki kirne enter hoti hain — is flow me mention hua.

🌧️ Average rainfall Q-type warning

  • Teacher ne bataya: bade region me rainfall fixed nahi hoti.
  • Exam me exact rainfall ke bajay region-based ya comparative question aa sakta hai.
  • Example: Satpura ke eastern part vs western part me rainfall comparison type question.
  • Average rainfall nature ke hisab se change ho sakti hai; padhai me region context important hai.
🔥 Exam Hot: Kaimur = Eastern Vindhyachal + Trikuta/Maihar + Yamuna-Son watershed.

⚡ Quick Recall

Q. Trikuta hill kis range me batayi gayi?
Kaimur range / Eastern Vindhyachal.
Q. Kaimur kin rivers ke beech watershed ka kaam karti hai?
Yamuna aur Son river ke beech.
Q. Vindhya naam ke myth me kaun se Rishi aaye?
Agastya Rishi.

🪨 Bhander Range + Western Vindh Range

2️⃣ Bhander Range / भांडेर श्रेणी

  • Vindhyachal ka middle part.
  • Approx Damoh se Raisen-Sehore tak.
  • Sagar, Panna, Chhatarpur jaise Bundelkhand ke areas isme include kiye.
  • Bhimbetka caves ko Bhander region me connect kiya.
  • Salkanpur / Bijasan / Bindwasini Mata temple, Sehore side ko Bhander connection diya.

🏔️ Goodwill Peak / सद्भावना शिखर

  • Damoh ke Sangrampur me batayi.
  • Names: Goodwill Peak, Sadbhavna Shikhar, Kalumar, Kumble Choti.
  • Height: approx 752 m.
  • Teacher ne ise MP ke context me Vindhyachal ki highest peak ke roop me yaad rakhne ko kaha.
  • Sigar peak (Malwa plateau, 881 m) ko lekar confusion bataya, par current exam answer ke liye Goodwill/Kalumar ko mark karne ko kaha.

3️⃣ Western Vindh / विंध श्रेणी

  • Vindhyachal ka western part.
  • Teacher ne kaha: kuch log is third part ko mention nahi karte, kyunki distance badh gayi / old range toot-gayi type situation hai.
  • Still revision ke liye isko likhna hai.
  • Major peaks/places: Kakri Bardi, Janapav, Asrafi hill.
  • Kakri Bardi aur Janapav: Indore side; Asrafi hill: Mandu/Dhar side.

⛩️ Temple reminders

  • Sharda Mata temple → Vindhyachal/Kaimur connection.
  • Salkanpur Bindwasini/Bijasan Mata temple → Bhander connection.
  • Teacher personally Salkanpur ka mention karte hain: 1100–1300+ stairs type high climb.
  • Hindi Granth Academy type note: kuch sources Narmada origin ko Vindhyachal se jodte hain because Maikal once touched/was part of Vindhyachal, but ab Satpura me include karke padhenge.
🧠 Memory Trick: Vindhyachal parts = Ka-Bha-Vi → Kaimur, Bhander, Vindh.

⚡ Quick Recall

Damoh se Raisen-Sehore tak.

🌲 Satpura Mountain Range — Block Mountain

📍 Basic facts

  • Satpura ki length approx 900 km batayi gayi.
  • Vindhyachal se newer hai, par Himalaya jaisa “new folded mountain” nahi.
  • Narmada aur Tapti rivers ke beech mountain range samjhaya.
  • Narmada valley ke end/edge ke baad Satpura start hone ka visual diya gaya.

🧱 Type: Block Mountain

  • Block Mountain / भ्रंशोत्थ पर्वत / अवरुद्ध पर्वत.
  • Rift/fault valley ke through mountain block ho jaye to block mountain.
  • Teacher ne “block karna / रोकना / अवरुद्ध करना” se concept samjhaya.
  • Narmada rift/valley relation ke through Satpura ko explain kiya.

🌙 Maikal Range — Eastern Satpura

📍 Extent

  • Satpura ka Eastern Part.
  • Amarkantak/Anuppur se start karke Seoni tak ka region.
  • Beech me Mandla, Dindori, Balaghat, Seoni side ko include kiya.
  • Balaghat me Lanji Hills / Lanji fort area ko Maikal ka part bataya.

🛕 Amarkantak + Narmada

  • Amarkantak = Satpura aur Vindhyachal ka meeting point.
  • Amarkantak height approx 1057 m / 1000 m se more batayi.
  • Yahin se Maa Narmada ka origin samjhaya.
  • Ek Taksh / Tarksh mountain/peak ka mention bhi kiya gaya.
  • Maikal ko Half-moon shaped / अर्ध चंद्राकार bataya.

🏞️ Other Maikal clues

  • Kirar Valley isi region me batayi.
  • Achanakmar-Amarkantak area aur biosphere reserve reference aaya.
  • Lanji Hills ko Balaghat me note karna hai.
  • Teacher ne kaha: ye minerals wala important part hai — maximum minerals ka hint diya.

🧠 Shape memory

Maikal ko half-moon / अर्धचंद्राकार yaad karne ke liye: “Maikal = Moon-like curve”. Map me Amarkantak se Seoni tak curve imagine karo.

🔥 Exam Hot: Amarkantak = Satpura + Vindhyachal meeting point; Narmada origin; Maikal eastern Satpura.

🏔️ Dhupgarh — Highest Peak

  • Dhupgarh ko MP aur Satpura dono ki highest peak bataya.
  • Height teacher ne 1350 m batayi; common sources 1352 m bhi likhte hain, par class notes me 1350 m retain kiya gaya.
  • Alternative names: Harvats Koot / हरवत्स कूट aur image discussion me Nagphani name bhi aaya.

🧱 Rajpipla Range — Western Satpura

📍 Western Satpura

  • Khandwa ke aas-paas se Burhanpur, Barwani side tak flow.
  • Aage Gujarat tak extension bataya; south side Maharashtra ka reference.
  • Satpura ka western part = Rajpipla Range.
  • Isko Satpura ka sabse zyada kata-fata / dissected part bola.

🚪 Burhanpur Pass / दर्रा

  • Burhanpur Pass / Burhanpur Darra = MP ka ek matra pass.
  • Pass = mountain ke ek side se dusri side jane ka रास्ता.
  • Location: Burhanpur district.
  • Question me “MP ka ekmatra darra” aaye to Burhanpur Pass.

🏰 Fort / hill regions

  • Gawilgarh: Khandwa-Betul ke beech ka region; Maharashtra connection bhi bataya gaya.
  • Bijagarh aur Asirgarh: Burhanpur.
  • Nadi/pahad district boundary se restrict nahi hote — jo district me padta hai, uska reference bhi ban sakta hai.

⛰️ Barwani side

  • Akhrani aur Barwani hills ka mention.
  • Chulgiri Hill: Rajpipla ki highest range/peak context me batayi.
  • Bawangaja Barwani Chulgiri se linked.
  • Barwani district me note karna hai.
🔥 Exam Hot: Rajpipla = western Satpura + most dissected + Burhanpur Pass = MP ka ek matra pass + Chulgiri/Bawangaja Barwani.
