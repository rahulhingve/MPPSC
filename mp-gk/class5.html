🌏 Peninsular India — MP ka broader setting

Peninsular / Praydweepiya Bharat: aisa landmass jo 3 sides se water se surrounded ho — West me Arabian Sea, South me Indian Ocean, East me Bay of Bengal.

🌊 3 Water Bodies

  • Arabian Sea — West
  • Indian Ocean / Hind Mahasagar — South
  • Bay of Bengal / Bengal ki Khadi — East

🗺️ MP ki Position

  • MP = Peninsular Plateau ka Northern Part.
  • Teacher ne MP ko Gondwana Land ki dharti bola.
  • South side me Maharashtra/Deccan Plateau context diya.

📏 Area Cue

Memory Trick: A-I-B = Arabian Sea, Indian Ocean, Bay of Bengal. MP = Peninsular plateau ka northern anchor.

📐 Plateau / पठार — Definition Clear

Hindi: Jab land ka koi hissa surrounding land se kuch upar uth jaye aur uski upper surface chikni, samatal aur sapat ho, to use Plateau / Pathar kehte hain.
English: When any part of land rises above the surrounding land and its upper surface is smooth and flat, it is called a plateau.

✅ Plateau ke signs

  • Nearby land se elevated.
  • Top smooth and flat.
  • Mountain jaisa conical/pointed top nahi.

📏 General Height

Teacher ne general plateau height 400-600 m ke around batayi; individual regions ki average height alag ho sakti hai.

🗺️ MP Link

MP ka major land plateau hai; class me 5 out of 7 divisions plateaus bataye gaye.

Memory Trick: U-S-F = Upar utha, Smooth top, Flat top.

🧩 Quick Recall

Q. Plateau aur mountain ka key difference? 👁️
Plateau ka top flat/smooth hota hai; mountain ka top conical/pointed ho sakta hai.
