🧠 1. Social Media ka Meaning

Media = Medium

  • Media ka simple meaning hai: माध्यम.
  • Detailed meaning: Means of Communication यानी जानकारी ko ek place se dusri place tak pahunchane ka माध्यम.
  • Example: Newspaper, TV, Internet, apps — sab information transfer karte hain.

Social Networking Site

  • Ek online platform / online मंच jahan duniya ke log connect kar sakte hain.
  • Users ek dusre se baat kar sakte hain.
  • Users files, photos, videos share kar sakte hain.
  • Users community create kar sakte hain.

🔑 Memory Trick

Social Media = Connect + Communicate + Community + Content Share

Yaad rakho: 4C Trick — Connect, Communicate, Community, Content.

🗂️ 4. Social Media ke 6 Main Types

Image: Wikimedia Commons — Martin Grandjean / SlvrKy, CC BY-SA 4.0. Concept: social platforms alag clusters/communities banate hain.

Image load nahi hui? Open source page.

1) Social Networking Sites

People ko connect, communicate, messages/photos/videos share karne ka platform. Examples: Facebook, LinkedIn, Telegram.

2) Bookmarking Sites

Important URLs ko store/bookmark karna. Examples: Delicious, StumbleUpon.

3) Social News Sites

Users news items, links, articles post karte hain; voting/commenting hoti hai. Examples: Reddit type platforms.

4) Media Sharing

Photos/videos upload/share. Instagram pehle photo-sharing tha; ab message/video bhi hai. Flickr, Pinterest, YouTube bhi याद रखें.

5) Microblogging

Limited words/characters mein message. Twitter शुरुआत में 140 characters; Koo bhi microblogging.

6) Blogs & Forums

Blog mein thoughts; Forum mein log discussion/comments karte hain. Mains ke 3-marker ke liye important.

🔥 Prelims vs Mains Importance
  • Prelims: Types ke direct examples — Pinterest = photo sharing, Quora = Q&A, Twitter/Koo = microblogging.
  • Mains: Blogs/forums, social media benefits/harms, regulation, fake news, privacy — short answers ke liye important.

⚡ Quick Recall

Q1. Bookmarking site kya karti hai?
Important URLs/websites ko store karti hai taki baad mein access kiya ja sake.
Q2. Twitter ka type kya hai?
Microblogging / micro-message sharing platform.
Q3. Blog aur Forum mein basic difference?
Blog: kisi user/group ke thoughts. Forum: multiple log discussion/comment karke apne views rakhte hain.

📌 Social Media Intermediary Duties

  • 180 days tak user records preserve/collect karna.
  • Cyber security incidents report karna.
  • Monthly compliance/reporting mechanism.
  • Primary source/originator identify karna — fake news/content tracing context.

🔥 Sensitive Content

  • Women se related inappropriate content ki complaint par 24 hours ke अंदर remove karna.
  • Non-compliance par safe harbour protection ka लाभ खत्म ho sakta hai.
  • Purpose: social media + OTT regulation.
Exam-ready Safe Harbour Note

Safe Harbour ka मतलब: platform ko user-generated content ke लिए legal protection milti hai, jab tak woh rules follow karta hai. Rules follow na karne par यह protection कमजोर/खत्म हो सकती है.

⚡ Quick Recall

Q1. IT Rules ke according user records kitne din tak rakhne ki बात class mein बताई गई?
180 days.
Q2. Women-related inappropriate material complaint par removal timeline?
24 hours ke अंदर.
Q3. Safe Harbour ka basic meaning?
Platform ko user-generated content ke liye legal protection; rules violate karne par यह benefit खत्म हो सकता है.

✅ 7. Social Media ke Benefits & Harms

✅ Benefits / लाभ

  • Duniya bhar ke logon se जुड़ने ka important साधन.
  • Mainstream se अलग लोगों की आवाज बन सकता है.
  • Business ke लिए important.
  • Employment/job opportunities ke लिए important.
  • Awareness aur current affairs mein help.

⚠️ Harms / हानि

  • Excessive use health ko negatively affect karta hai.
  • Depression aur mental health issues ki ओर ले जा सकता है.
  • Cyberbullying ko बढ़ावा.
  • Fake news aur hate speech spread.
  • Hacking, phishing aur privacy loss.
🧠 Mains Answer Structure

Intro: Social media online platform hai jo people ko connect karta hai.
Body: 3 benefits + 3 harms + regulation example.
Conclusion: Digital literacy, fact-checking, privacy protection aur balanced use.

Mnemonic: Benefits vs Harms

Benefits = C-B-J-A → Connect, Business, Jobs, Awareness.

Harms = H-D-C-F-P → Health, Depression, Cyberbullying, Fake news, Privacy loss.

⚡ Quick Recall

Q1. Social media marginalized people ke लिए कैसे useful ho sakta hai?
Ye unki voice बन सकता है jo society ki mainstream se अलग हैं.
Q2. Social media ke 3 harms बताओ.
Excessive use/health impact, depression, cyberbullying, fake news/hate speech, hacking/phishing, privacy loss.
Q3. Upcoming class topic kya bataya gaya?
Cyber Security — hacking, phishing jaise words detail mein cover honge.
