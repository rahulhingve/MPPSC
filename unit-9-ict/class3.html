⚖ ICT Laws & Policies

⭐ Exam Hotspot: IT Act 2000, IT Rules 2021, IT Policy 2012, National Digital Communication Policy 2018.
🔵 IT Act 2000

India mein IT, e-commerce, cyber crime regulation ka base act. 94 sections aur 13 chapters lecture mein highlighted.

Section 66A ko Shreya Singhal case ke baad struck down/removed context mein padhaya gaya.

❌ Exam Trap

IT Act ko sirf cyber crime mat samjho. E-commerce bhi IT Act 2000 se regulated bola gaya.

🌐 ICT Applications & Effects

⭐ Exam Hotspot: Lecture ne is section ko easy bola, but answer-writing ke liye examples ready rakho.
🔵 Daily Life Applications
  • Mobile phone aur internet use.
  • Computer-based work.
  • Digital services and online apps.
  • Education, banking, governance, entertainment.
✅ Positive Effects
  • World ko transform kiya.
  • Information access fast hua.
  • Services convenient hui.
  • Learning and communication democratize hua.
❌ Negative Effects
  • Health issues: screen time, posture, eyes.
  • Addiction/lut lag jana lecture ka biggest issue.
  • Privacy and cyber risks.
  • Fake information ka spread.

Quick Quiz

ICT ka biggest negative effect lecture mein kya bola?
Addiction/lut lag jana; health issue bhi highlighted.
Daily life mein common ICT tools?
Mobile, internet, computer, digital services/apps.
