⚠️ CSAT (Paper 2) ke baare mein important baat

Civil Service ke liye CSAT sirf qualifying hai (33% chahiye). Lekin Forest Service ke liye CSAT merit mein count hota hai! Isliye CSAT ko sirf 30-45 min roz do, ignore mat karo. 7 topics hain: Comprehension, Reasoning, Logical Ability, Decision Making, Mental Ability, Maths (Class 10 level), Hindi Comprehension.

Week 1–2 (Abhi)

Computer basics, AI, Robotics, Cyber Security, E-Governance, Social Media, Internet
🟠 Phase 2 — Science, Environment, Current Affairs
Week 3–5

🔵 Phase 5 — History (India + MP)
Week 13–16

Ancient, Medieval, Cultural Heritage, 19th-20th century reforms, Freedom Movement, Post-independence
Unit 2 — MP Itihas, Sanskriti, Sahitya
Rajvansh, Arts, Tribes, Folk music, Literary creators, World Heritage sites, Janjatiya personalities
🔁 Phase 6 — Grand Revision Cycle 1
Week 17–20

Sab 10 units ek baar fast revision. Har unit ko 2-2 din do. MCQ mock tests shuru karo. Previous year papers solve karo (2020, 2021, 2022, 2023). Galat answers pe extra focus.

🌳 Phase 7 — Forest Vaniki Add Karo (Section B)

⭐ 5 Golden Rules — Confusion Kabhi Nahi Hogi
📖
Ek Unit = 3 Din
Koi bhi unit ek sath khatam karo — beech mein mat chhodo. 3 din ek unit ko do, 4th day revision.
❌
Abhi Notes Mat Banao
Notes banane se time waste hota hai. Pehle 6 mahine sirf padho aur samjho. Notes Phase 6 mein banao.
📰
Current Affairs Daily Compulsory
Unit 8 ongoing hai — roz 30 min news. MP + National. Monthly ek jagah compile karo (diary ya app).
🔄
MCQ Practice Without Skip
Jo topic pada, uske MCQ roz solve karo. Galat jawab ka reason zaroor samjho — sirf answer dekhna nahi.
🌳
Forest Vaniki — 6 Mahine Baad
Abhi Vaniki mat padhna. Civil + Forest ka common GS foundation pehle mazboot karo. Vaniki month 7 se.
💤
6 Ghante Quality > 9 Ghante Baith Ke
Abhi 6 ghante productive hain — yahi kaafi hai. Dhire dhire badhega. Quality padhai hi result deti hai.

🎯 Sabse Badi Baat — Ye Plan Follow Karo

  • Subah uthke pehla kaam — Deep study block (2.5 hrs) karo, fresh mind hoti hai
  • Ek din mein ek hi unit — 2 units ek saath padhne se confusion hoti hai
  • Shanivaar — sirf revision, naya topic nahi
  • Ravivar — Rest ya Previous Year Papers — naya topic bilkul nahi
  • Monthly check — kitne units complete? MCQ accuracy kitni? Adjust karo
