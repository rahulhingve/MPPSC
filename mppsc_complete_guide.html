🔑 Unit 2, 4, 5 एक साथ क्यों?
ये तीनों Units में MP (Madhya Pradesh) का हिस्सा है + India का हिस्सा है। इनके लिए एक ही Book — Pariksha Dham — MP portion cover करती है। इसीलिए Video में इन्हें एक साथ mention किया गया। लेकिन ये तीन अलग-अलग Units हैं: Unit 2 = MP History/Culture, Unit 4 = MP Geography, Unit 5 = MP + India Polity.
यह Official MPPSC Prelims 2026 का Paper I (General Studies) का पूरा Syllabus है। Line by Line इसी से पढ़ो — इससे बाहर कुछ नहीं पूछा जाएगा।
Unit 1 — India Level

📜 History of India

  • Ancient Indian Knowledge Tradition — Vedas, Upanishads, Aranyakas, Brahmanas, Shaddarshan, Smritis, Sabha-Samiti, Republics, Varna-Ashrama, Purushartha, Panch Mahayagya, Karma, Bodhisattva, Tirthankara
  • Salient features of Ancient & Medieval India — Administrative, Social, Economic Systems
  • India's Cultural Heritage — Art forms, Literature, Festivals, Events
  • Social & Religious Reform Movements of 19th & 20th Century
  • Independence Struggle & Indian National Movement
  • Integration & Reorganization of India after Independence
Unit 2 — MP Level

🏛️ History, Culture & Literature of Madhya Pradesh

  • Major Events & Major Dynasties of Madhya Pradesh
  • Contribution of MP in Freedom Movement
  • Major Arts & Sculpture of Madhya Pradesh
  • Major Tribes & Dialects of MP
  • Major Festivals, Folk Music, Folk Arts, Folk Literature of MP
  • Important Literary Creators of MP and their Works
  • Major Tourist Places — Religious, Cultural, Archaeological importance in MP
  • World Heritage Sites in Madhya Pradesh
  • Important Tribal Personalities of Madhya Pradesh
Unit 3 — India Level

🌏 Geography of India

  • Mountains, Hills, Plateaus, Rivers, and Lakes
  • Climate phenomena — El Niño, La Niña, Southern Oscillation, Western Disturbance, Climate Change
  • Natural Resources — Forest, Minerals, Water Resources
  • Major Crops, Food Security, Green Revolution, Second Green Revolution strategies
  • Conventional & Non-Conventional Energy Sources
  • Natural Hazards & Disasters in India, Major Cyclones
  • Population Growth, Distribution, Density, Rural-Urban Migration
Unit 4 — MP Level

🗺️ Geography of Madhya Pradesh

  • Forest, Forest Produce, Rivers, Hills, and Plateaus of MP
  • Climate — Seasons, Temperature, Rainfall
  • Natural Resources — Soils and Major Mineral Resources
  • Major Crops, Water Resources, Irrigation & Irrigation Projects
  • Conventional & Non-Conventional Sources of Energy in MP
  • Major Industries of Madhya Pradesh
  • Population Growth, Distribution, Density, Urbanization in MP
Unit 5 — India + MP

⚖️ Constitutional System of India & Madhya Pradesh

  • Constituent Assembly
  • Federal Executive — President, Parliament
  • Supreme Court & Judicial System
  • Constitutional Amendments
  • Fundamental Rights & Duties, Directive Principles of State Policy (DPSP)
  • National & Regional Constitutional/Statutory Commissions & Institutions
  • Constitutional System of MP — Governor, Cabinet, Legislative Assembly, High Court
  • Three-Tier Panchayati Raj & Civil Administration in MP
  • Good Governance in Madhya Pradesh
Unit 6 — India + MP

💰 Economy of India & Madhya Pradesh

  • Current Situation of MP in Indian Economy
  • Population & Human Resources Development — Education, Health, Skills in MP
  • Progress in Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in MP
  • Agriculture, Industry, MSME, Infrastructure Development in MP
  • Aatmanirbhar MP, One District One Product (ODOP)
  • Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) in MP
  • Recent Trends in Indian Economy — Agriculture, Industry, Service sectors
  • Financial Institutions — RBI, Commercial Banks, SEBI, NBFCs
  • India's Foreign Trade Policies — G-20, SAARC, ASEAN
Unit 7 — India Level ✅ High Priority

🔬 Science, Environment & Health

  • Preliminary Knowledge of Main Branches of Science
  • Important Indian Scientific Research Institutions & their Achievements
  • Satellite & Space Technology Achievements of India
  • Structure of Human Body
  • Nutrition, Food, Nutrients & Malnutrition
  • Genetic Diseases — Sickle Cell Anemia: Cause, Effect, Diagnosis, Programs
  • Health Policy & Programs, Infectious Diseases & Prevention, Health Indicators
  • Sustainable Development & SDGs
  • Environmental Factors, Biodiversity & Ecosystem
  • Pollution, Natural Calamities & Management
Unit 8 — All Levels ✅ High Priority

📰 Current Affairs — International, National & MP

  • International Current Events (Global Affairs, International Relations)
  • National Current Events (India-level News, Schemes, Awards, etc.)
  • Current Events of Madhya Pradesh (MP-specific News, Developments)
Unit 9 — India Level ✅ High Priority

💻 Information & Communication Technology

  • Basic Knowledge of Computer (Hardware, Software, Generations)
  • Electronics, Information & Communication Technology
  • Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Cyber Security
  • E-Governance
  • Internet & Social Networking Platforms
Unit 10 — MP Level ✅ High Priority

🏕️ Tribes of MP — Heritage, Folk Culture & Literature

  • Geographical Distribution of Tribes in MP, Constitutional Provisions for Tribes
  • Major Tribes of MP, PVTGs (Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups), Tribal Welfare Programs
  • Tribal Culture of MP — Customs, Traditions, Special Arts, Festivals, Dialects, Literature
  • MP Tribals' Contribution to Freedom Movement & Tribal Personalities of MP
  • Important Institutes, Museums & Publications related to MP Tribes
  • Folk Culture & Literature of Madhya Pradesh

🎯 Reading Order — किस क्रम में पढ़ें?

✅ Phase 1 — पहले ये Units (आसान + High Scoring)

  • Unit 10 — Janjati: MP-specific, कम Syllabus, Easy to score
  • Unit 9 — Computer: Fixed Topics, Short Syllabus, Scoring
  • Unit 7 — Science: Fixed Topics, Pariksha Dham से Point-to-Point
  • Unit 4 — MP Geography: MP-specific, Pariksha Dham काफी है
  • Unit 2 — MP History/Culture: MP-specific, Pariksha Dham काफी है

⚡ Phase 2 — इसके बाद

  • Unit 5 — Polity (India + MP): India Polity = Shri Rama Academy, MP = Pariksha Dham
  • Unit 8 — Current Affairs: Daily basis पर Kumar Gaurav Sir, Monthly Tathyavan

📌 Phase 3 — Vast Units (Time दो)

  • Unit 1 — India History: Coaching Notes (Akar/Utkarsh/Drishti) से पढ़ो
  • Unit 3 — India Geography: Shubham Gupta Sir + Coaching Notes

⏳ Phase 4 — Exam से 2-3 महीने पहले

  • Unit 6 — Economy: तब Market की सबसे Updated Book/Notes लो
  • Unit 8 — Current Affairs: Tathyavan Magazine की Latest Issue लो
  • सभी Units का Revision करो
⚠️ Common Mistakes — ये मत करो

❌ गलतियाँ जो Avoid करनी हैं

  • Laxmikant, Spectrum जैसी मोटी Books — 1 साल या कम समय में बिलकुल मत पढ़ो
  • NCERT की जरूरत नहीं — MPPSC अपने Fixed Topics पूछता है, UPSC की तरह नहीं
  • UPSC और MPPSC को Compare मत करो — Syllabus और Level बिलकुल अलग है
  • Random YouTube Videos देखना — सिर्फ Full Playlist वाले Channels follow करो
  • Unit 6 (Economy) को अभी से ही पूरा पढ़ना — Data outdated हो जाएगा
  • Subject-wise पढ़ना — हमेशा Syllabus Line by Line & Unit by Unit पढ़ो
💡 Smart Tips

✅ ये करो

  • पहले MPPSC Official Syllabus PDF Download करो — mppsc.mp.gov.in पर जाओ
  • हर Unit का Syllabus print करो, पढ़ते समय हर point tick करते जाओ
  • Last 4 Units (7, 8, 9, 10) पर special focus — इनमें कम समय में ज्यादा Units cover होती हैं
  • MP-specific Content (Unit 2, 4, 10) — यह सिर्फ MPPSC में पूछा जाता है, यहाँ compete कम है
  • Paper II (CSAT) के लिए ज्यादा time मत दो — सिर्फ 33% चाहिए qualifying के लिए
  • 10th/11th/12th में हो → पहले Board पूरा करो, फिर तैयारी शुरू करो
