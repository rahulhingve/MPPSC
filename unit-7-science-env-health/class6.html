🔁 Recap — Ecology vs Ecosystem

📌 Ecology / पारिस्थितिकी

  • Ecology ek study / अध्ययन hai.
  • Isme kisi special जीव-समुदाय ka अपने environment ke saath interaction / अंतःक्रिया study hota hai.
  • Word origin: Oikos = घर/आवास, Logos = अध्ययन.
  • Class recap ke hisaab se Ernest Haeckel ka naam yaad rakhna hai.

🌍 Ecosystem / पारिस्थितिक तंत्र

  • Jis area mein ecology ka study hota hai, us area ko ecosystem bola.
  • Teacher ne ecosystem ko environment ka chhota hissa aur nature ki functional unit bataya.
  • Ecosystems ek doosre se interconnected ho sakte hain.
  • Types recap: Natural aur Artificial.
🔥 Exam Hot: “Ecology study hai, Ecosystem study ka area/functional unit hai” — difference confuse nahi karna.
🧠 Memory Trick

Eco-logy = Logic/Study aur Eco-system = System/Area. “-logy” sunte hi subject/study yaad karo.

Image: Wikimedia Commons, Thompsma/Kayau, CC BY-SA 3.0 — simplified ecosystem energy and nutrient flow diagram.

Quick Recall

Q1. Ecology ka basic meaning kya hai?
जीव-समुदाय aur environment ke interaction ka अध्ययन.
Q2. Ecosystem ko class mein nature ki kya unit bola gaya?
Nature ki functional unit.
Q3. Oikos aur Logos ka meaning?
Oikos = घर/आवास; Logos = अध्ययन.

🧬 Ecosystem Components — जैविक & अजैविक

Core Rule: Environment ke jaise ecosystem ke bhi do components hote hain — Biotic / जैविक aur Abiotic / अजैविक.

🌱 Biotic Components / जैविक घटक

  • Jisme jaan hoti hai — living components.
  • Biosphere ke living organisms ko ecosystem mein teen roles mein arrange karte hain:
  • Producer / उत्पादक
  • Consumer / उपभोक्ता
  • Decomposer / अपघटन कर्ता

🪨 Abiotic Components / अजैविक घटक

  • Non-living components: physical factors.
  • Environment recap: atmosphere, hydrosphere, lithosphere.
  • Ecosystem explanation: Inorganic elements, Organic elements, Climate.
  • Inorganic = jisme carbon nahi; Organic = jisme carbon quantity ho.

☀️ Autotroph, Heterotroph & Photosynthesis

🌿 Autotrophic / स्वपोषी संघटक

  • Jo apna food khud banate hain.
  • Photosynthesis / प्रकाश संश्लेषण karte hain.
  • Green plants aur algae type green vegetation.
  • Producer group ko swposhi bhi bol sakte hain.

🦌 Heterotrophic / परपोषी संघटक

  • Jo apna food khud nahi banate.
  • Food ke liye doosre organisms par dependent.
  • Consumers aur decomposers is broad sense mein doosron se nutrition lete hain.
Photosynthesis formula-style recall:
Sunlight ☀️+CO₂+H₂O+Minerals+Chlorophyll→Glucose / Food
  • Leaves mein chlorophyll hota hai.
  • Roots soil se water + mineral salts kheenchti hain.
  • Atmosphere se carbon dioxide milti hai.
  • Sunlight energy se chemical synthesis hota hai aur glucose banta hai.
Mnemonic: “Su-CO-Wa-Mi-Chlo = Food Glow” — Sunlight, CO₂, Water, Minerals, Chlorophyll se glucose/food.
Image: Wikimedia Commons, At09kg/Wattcle/Nefronus, CC BY-SA 4.0 — photosynthesis process diagram.

Quick Recall

Q1. Autotroph ka Hindi term?
स्वपोषी.
Q2. Photosynthesis mein leaves ka important pigment?
Chlorophyll.
Q3. Plants ko food banane ke liye class mein kaun-si 4 main cheezein batayi?
Sunlight, CO₂, water, mineral salts — chlorophyll ki presence mein.

🔗 Food Chain — खाद्य श्रृंखला

Definition: Aisi खाने की chain jisme ek organism doosre organism se food/energy leta hai, use Food Chain / खाद्य श्रृंखला kehte hain.
🔥 Exam Hot: First trophic level / प्रथम पोषक स्तर = Producer / Green plants. “Primary consumer” mat mark karna.

Correct Sequence

Producer 🌱→Primary Consumer 🦌→Secondary Consumer 🐍→Tertiary / Apex 🦅→Decomposer 🍄

Example 1

Grass → Grasshopper → Frog → Snake → Eagle

घास producer hai; टिड्डा primary consumer; मेंढक/snake/carnivore levels; eagle apex.

Example 2

Grass/Tree → Deer → Lion → Vulture/Eagle

Herbivore directly producer par depend; carnivore indirectly producer par depend.

Why chain break dangerous?

Teacher ne bola: Food chain se ek bhi link hatane par poora chain disturb hota hai; food web aur ecosystem bhi disturb ho sakta hai. Isliye ecosystem mein har organism ka role important hai.

Image: Wikimedia Commons, Thompsma/Kayau, CC BY-SA 3.0 — energy flow and nutrient movement model.

Quick Recall

Q1. First trophic level kaun hota hai?
Producer / Green plants.
Q2. Grass → Grasshopper → Frog → Snake → Eagle mein grass ka role?
Producer.
Q3. Food chain ka link break hone se kya hoga?
Chain aur ecosystem disturb hoga.

🕸️ Food Web — खाद्य जाल

Definition: Bahut sari food chains jab आपस में जुड़ती/क्रॉस होती hain, to Food Web / खाद्य जाल banta hai.
🔥 Exam Hot: Food web ka another name class mein समिश्र खाद्य श्रृंखला / mixed chain bataya gaya.

🧩 Web ka idea

  • Ek chain: Grass → Mouse → Owl
  • Doosri chain: Grass → Mouse → Hawk
  • Teesri chain: Grass → Rabbit → Fox
  • Jab links mix hote hain, web ban jata hai.

🌊 Aquatic example

  • Algae/plants → small fish → big fish → bird
  • Aquatic web mein decomposers bhi nutrient recycle karte hain.
  • Multiple chains ek hi ecosystem mein chal sakti hain.
Image: Wikimedia Commons, Kestin Schulz, Mariya W. Smit, Lydie Herfort and Holly M. Simon / Missouri Department of Conservation, CC BY-SA 4.0 — aquatic food web example.

Quick Recall

Q1. Food web kaise banta hai?
Multiple food chains ke आपस में जुड़ने/क्रॉस होने se.
Q2. Food web ka doosra naam?
समिश्र खाद्य श्रृंखला / mixed food chain.
Q3. Aquatic chain ka class example?
Plants/algae → small fish → big fish → bird.

⚡ Energy Flow & Lindeman 10% Rule

⚡ Energy Flow Facts

  • Energy flow unidirectional / एक दिशी hota hai.
  • Flow bottom se top ki ओर: Producer → Consumers.
  • Start abiotic system se hota hai: sunlight.
  • Then biotic system: plants, herbivores, carnivores.
  • Solar energy primary source hai.

🔥 Energy Loss

  • Energy conversion mein hamesha loss hota hai.
  • Heat ke form mein environment mein release hoti hai.
  • Body organs/digestion/metabolism bhi energy use karte hain.
  • Isliye 100% energy next level tak nahi jaati.
🔥 Exam Hot: Energy flow ka direction MCQ: Abiotic → Biotic, because sunlight se producer tak flow start hota hai.

Lindeman 10% Rule / ऊर्जा प्रवाह का 10% नियम

Lindeman / Lindman, 1942: Har trophic level se next trophic level tak roughly sirf 10% energy transfer hoti hai.
Producer 10000 kJ→Herbivore 1000 kJ→Carnivore 100 kJ→Apex 10 kJ→Next 1 kJ
Class example explained

Teacher ne example diya: agar plants/producers mein 10000 kJ energy hai, to primary consumer ko 1000 kJ, secondary ko 100 kJ, tertiary/apex ko 10 kJ milta hai. Har next step pe 10% only.

Image: Wikimedia Commons, Swiggity.Swag.YOLO.Bro, CC BY-SA 4.0 — energy pyramid showing energy decline across trophic levels.

Quick Recall

Q1. Energy flow one-way hota hai ya cyclic?
One-way / एक दिशी.
Q2. 10% rule kisne diya aur kab?
Lindeman/Lindman, 1942.
Q3. 10000 kJ ke baad primary consumer ko kitna?
1000 kJ.
Q4. Energy flow start kis system se?
Abiotic system — sunlight — se biotic system ki ओर.

📊 Biomass & Number Pyramids

🌳 Biomass Pyramid — Terrestrial

  • Example: one big tree ecosystem.
  • Tree ka biomass sabse zyada.
  • Birds ka total biomass tree se kam.
  • Parasites aur hyperparasites aur kam.
  • Shape: Upright / सीधा.

🌊 Biomass Pyramid — Aquatic

  • Ocean example: phytoplankton/plants ka biomass least.
  • Crustaceans → small fish → big fish biomass higher.
  • Shape: Inverted / उल्टा.
  • Conclusion: biomass pyramid dono shape le sakta hai.

🌳 Number Pyramid — Tree

  • One tree → many birds → more parasites → even more hyperparasites.
  • Numbers increase upward.
  • Shape: Inverted / उल्टा.

🌾 Number Pyramid — Grassland

  • Grass many → grasshoppers fewer → frogs fewer → snakes fewer → peacock/eagle fewer.
  • Numbers decrease upward.
  • Shape: Upright / सीधा.

👣 Ecological Footprint — पारिस्थितिकी पदछाप

Meaning: मनुष्य ko apni lifestyle smoothly chalane ke liye jitni ecological resources/components ki आवश्यक मात्रा chahiye, use ecological footprint bola.

Resources in footprint

  • Forest products
  • Carbon footprint
  • Crop land
  • Building/built-up land
  • Fisheries
  • Land, ocean, animals, agriculture, transport, industry — lifestyle resources.

Measurement facts

  • Mापन: मनुष्य द्वारा उत्सर्जित carbon ki quantity se.
  • Unit: भूमंडली हेक्टेयर / Global hectare.
  • Organic matter mein carbon hota hai.
  • Wood/clothes/biological material burn karne par carbon compounds release hote hain.
🔥 Exam Hot: Ecological footprint ki unit = Global hectare / भूमंडली हेक्टेयर.
Image: Wikimedia Commons, Global Footprint Network Open Data Platform, CC BY-SA 4.0 — ecological footprint map.

Quick Recall

Q1. Ecological footprint ka basic meaning?
Lifestyle chalane ke liye required ecological resources ki amount.
Q2. Footprint measurement kis se linked bataya?
Human-emitted carbon ki quantity.
Q3. Unit kya hai?
Global hectare / भूमंडली हेक्टेयर.

🌊 Misc Facts — Eucalyptus, Lentic/Lotic, Ecotone & Movements

🌳 Eucalyptus — पर्यावरणीय शत्रु

  • Eucalyptus ka tree class mein पर्यावरणीय शत्रु bola gaya.
  • Reason: अत्यधिक water uptake / जल ग्रहण शक्ति.
  • Soil water level ko गिरा deta hai.
  • Soil fertility / उर्वरता par negative effect.
  • Australia mein bahutayat, India mein bhi kaafi.
