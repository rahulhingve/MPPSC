🧩 Recap: Environment Basics

Lecture ne last class ka base repeat kiya, kyunki protection samajhne ke liye pehle environment ke components clear hone chahiye.

Environment ka meaning

  • परि + आवरण: jo humein chaaro taraf se ghere hue hai.
  • 1986 Act ke according: physical aur biological conditions plus unki interaction ko environment kaha gaya.
  • Components ko hi environmental factors bhi maan sakte hain.

Do main components

  • Biotic / जैविक: biosphere, plants, animals, micro-organisms.
  • Abiotic / अजैविक: lithosphere, hydrosphere, atmosphere.
  • Teacher ne man-made environment bhi factor bataya: industrial complex, transport, commercial areas.
Component Examples Memory Hook
जैविक / Biotic जीव-जंतु, पेड़-पौधे, सूक्ष्म जीव, जीवाणु, विषाणु, कवक Jisme life hai, wo biotic.
अजैविक / Abiotic वायु, जल, स्थल, मृदा, खनिज, शैल Jisme jaan nahi, wo abiotic.
मानव निर्मित कृषि उत्पाद क्षेत्र, औद्योगिक संकुल, परिवहन, वाणिज्यिक संकुल Human activity se bana environment.

🧠 Quick Recall

Environment ka shabdik arth kya hai?

परि + आवरण, yani jo humein chaaro taraf se ghere hue hai.

Biotic aur abiotic ka basic difference?

Biotic mein life hoti hai; abiotic mein life nahi hoti.

Atmosphere, hydrosphere, lithosphere kis category mein aate hain?

Abiotic / अजैविक components.

⚠️ Protection ki Need

Teacher ne Earth ko ghar ke example se samjhaya: ghar ganda ho to rehna mushkil hota hai; Earth bhi shared home hai, isliye protection zaroori hai.

Why protection?

  • Future generations ke liye Earth secure rakhni hai.
  • Human activities se environment mein negative changes aa rahe hain.
  • Climate change se disaster risk badhta hai.
  • Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam: Earth ko common home ki tarah treat karo.

Major impacts discussed

  • जलवायु परिवर्तन / Climate Change
  • वैश्विक तापन / Global Warming
  • Greenhouse gases ka increase
  • Glacier melting and sea level rise
  • Ozone layer depletion and UV radiation risk
🔥 Exam Hot: Teacher ne bola ki protection se questions banenge; environmental factors ke saath conservation bhi padho.

🧠 Quick Recall

Global warming ka simple cause lecture mein kya bataya?

Greenhouse gases badhne se average temperature normal se zyada ho jata hai.

Sea level rise ka link kya hai?

Glaciers melt hote hain, ocean level badhta hai, coastal/land areas doob sakte hain.

Ozone layer humein kis radiation se bachati hai?

Ultraviolet / पराबैंगनी radiation.

🚲 Stockholm Conference 1972

Environment protection par global level ka first major conference. Lecture mein isko environment ka Magna Carta bola gaya.

Core facts

  • Year: 1972
  • Date: 5 June 1972
  • Place: Stockholm, Sweden
  • Countries: 119 countries
  • Theme idea: climate/environment protection ke liye global rules.

UNEP

  • UNEP: United Nations Environment Programme.
  • Hindi: संयुक्त राष्ट्र पर्यावरण कार्यक्रम.
  • Established: 1972, Stockholm Conference ki achievement.
  • Headquarters: Nairobi, Kenya.
  • Award: Global 500 Award, environment work ke liye.
🔥 Exam Hot: Environment ka Magna Carta = Stockholm Conference 1972. World Environment Day = 5 June; first celebrated in 1973.

🧠 Quick Recall

Stockholm Conference kis year mein hua?

1972.

UNEP ka HQ kahan hai?

Nairobi, Kenya.

World Environment Day pehli baar kab manaya gaya?

5 June 1973.

🌎 Earth Summit 1992 | Rio Conference

Stockholm ke 20 saal baad review hua aur naye bodies/conventions banaye gaye. Lecture mein names: Earth Summit, पृथ्वी शिखर सम्मेलन, Rio Conference. Note: official terminology mein Rio+20 generally 2012 follow-up ke liye use hota hai; 1992 wala exam fact Earth Summit/Rio Conference hai.

Fact Revision Point
Year 1992
Dates 3 June to 14 June 1992
Place Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
Main aim Stockholm decisions ka review, sustainable development, environment protection.
Main achievement CBD, UNFCCC, UNCCD; Agenda 21; Rio Declaration; Forest Principles.

Three bodies

  • UNFCCC: climate change par work.
  • CBD: biological diversity ko conserve karna.
  • UNCCD: desertification / मरुस्थलीकरण ko combat karna.

Agenda 21

  • Earth Summit se related 21 points/goals ka document.
  • Forest Principles / वनीकरण.
  • Sustainable Development ko dhyan mein rakha gaya.
  • Rio Declaration implementation.
  • Teacher ne bataya: ye non-binding / गैर बाध्यकारी tha.
1972 Stockholm→20-year review→1992 Earth Summit→Agenda 21→1997 Earth Summit +5, New York
🔥 Exam Hot: Agenda 21 kis se related hai? Earth Summit / Rio Conference / पृथ्वी शिखर सम्मेलन 1992.

🧠 Quick Recall

Earth Summit kahan hua?

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

CBD kis topic se related hai?

Biological diversity / जैव विविधता.

UNCCD ka focus kya hai?

Combat Desertification, yani मरुस्थलीकरण ko rokna.

🌡️ UNFCCC and COP Milestones

Lecture ke hisab se UNFCCC Earth Summit ki key achievement hai. Iske member countries climate change control ke liye har saal COP meeting karte hain.

UNFCCC facts

  • Full form: United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change.
  • Hindi sense: जलवायु परिवर्तन पर संयुक्त राष्ट्र रूपरेखा सम्मेलन.
  • Created: 1992.
  • Effective: 1994.
  • First COP: 1995, Berlin, Germany.

COP meaning

  • COP: Conference of Parties.
  • UNFCCC member countries ki annual climate meeting.
  • Goal: climate change ko control karna, emissions reduce karna.
COP / Agreement Year Place Exam Point
COP 1 1995 Berlin, Germany First COP.
COP 3 / Kyoto Protocol 1997 Kyoto, Japan Carbon emissions reduction, UNFCCC se related.
COP 8 2002 New Delhi, India Poor countries ki development needs aur climate change reduction.
COP 21 / Paris Agreement 2015 Paris, France Temperature rise ko 2°C se neeche, 1.5°C tak limit karne ka effort.
COP 25 2019 Madrid, Spain Lecture list ka important COP.
No COP 2020 Not held Special fact: 2020 mein COP nahi hui.
COP 26 2021 Glasgow, UK Post-2020 COP.
COP 27 2022 Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt Recent COP in lecture.
COP 28 2023 Dubai, UAE 30 Nov to 12 Dec 2023; transcript mein UAE/Abu Dhabi bola gaya, official city Dubai hai.
🔥 Exam Hot: Kyoto Protocol = COP 3, 1997, UNFCCC, climate change and carbon emission reduction.

🧠 Quick Recall

UNFCCC kab effective hua?

1994.

COP 1 kahan hua?

Berlin, Germany, 1995.

Paris Agreement ka COP number?

COP 21.

2020 ka special COP fact?

2020 mein COP organize nahi hui.

🛡️ Ozone Layer and Montreal Protocol

Lecture mein ozone depletion ko environment protection ka important part bataya gaya, kyunki ozone layer harmful UV radiation ko rokne mein help karti hai.

Ozone layer role

  • Sun se aane wali harmful ultraviolet rays ko rokna.
  • UV rays directly aayen to skin cancer aur plants damage ka risk.
  • Ozone depletion = ओजोन परत का क्षरण.

Montreal Protocol

  • Year: 1987.
  • Related to: Ozone layer protection.
  • Ozone-depleting substances ko control karne ka global effort.

🧠 Quick Recall

Montreal Protocol kis se related hai?

Ozone layer protection / ozone depletion control.

Ozone layer ka major function?

UV / पराबैंगनी radiation se protection.

World Ozone Day kab manaya jata hai?

16 September.

NGT

  • Full form: National Green Tribunal.
  • Hindi: राष्ट्रीय हरित न्यायाधिकरण.

NEERI and NEAA

  • Place: Nagpur, Maharashtra; Year: 1958.
  • NEAA: National Environmental Appellate Authority.
  • NEAA: 1997 Act; Environment Protection Act 1986 violations/appeals related hearing.

📅 Important Environment Days

Teacher ne ek linked story di: pehle mitti ko nam karo, phir tree lagao, next day water do, phir Earth green hogi, biodiversity aayegi, environment banega, ozone protect hoga.

🧠 Memory Trick

नमी → वन → जल → पृथ्वी → जैव विविधता → पर्यावरण → ओजोन

Socho: 2 Feb ko wetland/nami, 21 March ko forest, 22 March ko water, 22 April ko Earth, 22 May biodiversity, 5 June environment, 16 September ozone.

Date Day Hook
2 February World Wetlands Day / आर्द्रभूमि दिवस Mitti mein नमी banao.
21 March World Forest Day / वन दिवस Tree lagao, forest banao.
22 March World Water Day / जल दिवस Tree ko next day paani do.
22 April Earth Day / पृथ्वी दिवस Forest se Earth green ho gayi.
22 May International Biodiversity Day / जैव विविधता दिवस Plants aur animals ki variety.
5 June World Environment Day / पर्यावरण दिवस Pura environment hara-bhara.
16 September World Ozone Day / विश्व ओजोन दिवस Ozone layer ko protect karna.
🔥 Exam Hot: 5 June = World Environment Day; first celebrated on 5 June 1973. 16 September = World Ozone Day.

🧠 Quick Recall

World Water Day?

22 March.

World Biodiversity Day?

22 May.

Mnemonic ka order?

नमी → वन → जल → पृथ्वी → जैव विविधता → पर्यावरण → ओजोन.
