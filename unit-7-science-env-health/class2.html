🌳 Science Tree

Science ka matlab organized knowledge hai. Yeh real experiences, experiments aur observations par based hota hai.

🔵 Definition

Science = physical world, nature aur universe ka organized knowledge. Hinglish trick: "Systematic knowledge hi Science hai."

🧱 Subject Matter

Nature, Universe, Matter aur Energy. Matter space occupy karta hai aur mass hota hai. Energy ko feel karte hain, weigh nahi.

LevelBranchMemory Hook
RootScienceEk bada ped.
Main 1Natural ScienceNature, matter, life ka study.
Main 2Social ScienceHuman society, economics, politics, sociology, psychology.
Natural ScienceMaterial ScienceNon-living matter. Isme Physics + Chemistry.
Natural ScienceLife ScienceLiving beings. Isme Biology.
ScienceNatural Science + Social Science
Natural ScienceMaterial Science + Life Science

Life ScienceBiology: Zoology, Botany, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Genetics etc.
❌ Common MistakePsychology ko Biology mein mat daalna. Lecture ke hisab se Psychology Social Science hai, jabki Neuroscience Biology mein aata hai.
⭐ Most Asked in ExamsScience ki do badi branches: Natural Science aur Social Science. Natural Science ke andar Material Science aur Life Science.

🧠 Quick Quiz

1. Science ki simple definition kya hai?
Systematic / organized knowledge based on observation and experiment.
2. Natural Science ke do parts kaun se hain?
Material Science aur Life Science.
3. Matter aur Energy mein basic difference?
Matter ka mass aur volume hota hai. Energy ko feel karte hain, mass-volume nahi hota.

⚡ Energy Examples

Light, heat, sound, electricity. Inka mass-volume nahi hota, par effect feel hota hai.

⚗️ Chemistry Branches

Chemistry bhi Material Science ka part hai. Exam mein mostly branch name aur study area match hota hai.

⭐ Exam HotspotSchool wali teen branches pehle pakdo: Physical Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry.
BranchStudy AreaQuick Hook
Physical Chemistry / भौतिक रसायनMatter-energy relationship, reactions, laws, principles, changes.Physics + Chemistry ka mix.
Organic Chemistry / कार्बनिक रसायनCarbon compounds, hydrocarbons, biological carbon compounds.Organic = Carbon.
Inorganic Chemistry / अकार्बनिक रसायनNon-carbon / mineral / metal / rock type substances.Inorganic = without carbon focus.
Industrial ChemistryLarge-scale production of useful goods using chemicals.Factory + chemical process.
Analytical ChemistryIdentification, quantity, volume and analysis of substances.Analyze = identify + measure.
Medicinal ChemistryComposition and preparation methods of medicines.Medicine ka formula.
RadiochemistryRadioactive substances, radiations and chemical uses.Radiation in Chemistry.
ElectrochemistryElectrolysis, electrodes, cells, batteries, chemical current flow.Current + solution.
Geochemistry / भू रसायनEarth minerals, useful substances, extraction-related methods.Geo = Earth.
Photochemistry / प्रकाश रसायनChemical effects and processes involving light.Photo = Light.

🧪 Organic Examples

Hydrocarbons: methane, ethane, propane. Carbon allotropes: diamond, graphite, fullerene.

🔋 Electrochemistry

Electrodes + solution + current. Dry cell aur battery examples yaad rakhna.

💊 Medicinal Chemistry

Tablet ya syrup kis composition se bana, aur kaise bana. Yeh medicinal chemistry hai.

❌ Common MistakeOrganic ka matlab "living" sirf nahi. Exam shortcut: carbon compound present ho to Organic Chemistry.
Mnemonic: P-O-I = Physical, Organic, Inorganic. Chemistry ki base 3 शाखाएं.
⭐ Most Asked in ExamsElectrochemistry = electrolysis, electrodes, cells aur batteries. Geochemistry = Earth ke minerals and extraction.

🧠 Quick Quiz

1. Carbon compounds ka study?
Organic Chemistry / कार्बनिक रसायन.
2. Medicines ke composition aur making methods ka study?
Medicinal Chemistry / औषधी रसायन.
3. Earth minerals aur useful substances ka chemical study?
Geochemistry / भू रसायन.

🧬 Biology Branches

Biology ko Life Science bola gaya. Living beings aur unki processes, chemicals, heredity, behavior, environment sab ismein aate hain.

⭐ Exam HotspotBiology ka broad split: Zoology = animals, Botany = plants. Father facts end mein yaad karo.
BranchStudy AreaMemory Trick
Zoology / प्राणी विज्ञानAnimals ka study.Zoo = animals.
Botany / वनस्पति विज्ञानPlants ka study.Botanical garden = plants.
Biochemistry / जैव रसायनLiving beings ke chemical components and chemical processes.Body chemicals: HCl, saliva.
Biometrics / जैव सांख्यिकीBiological actions/data ka mathematical analysis.Bio + Maths.
Biophysics / जैव भौतिकीPhysics principles ke basis par biological processes.Bio + Physics.
Molecular Biology / आणविक जीव विज्ञानLiving beings ka chemical/molecular level study.Molecule level.
Microbiology / सूक्ष्म जीव विज्ञानMicroorganisms: bacteria, viruses, fungi.Micro = very small.
Genetic EngineeringGenetic material ke use/combination se new hereditary traits wale organisms.Genes design karna.
Physiology / कार्यिकीOrgans aur whole body ke functions and mechanisms.Function = physiology.
BionicsBiological functions, properties and methods ka study.Bio systems ka method.
BionomicsOrganisms and environment ka combined study.Bio + environment.
EcologyEcosystem / environment relationship.Eco = environment.
BiomeLife rules ka study, lecture term: बायोमी.Life ke rules.
EthologyHumans and animals ke behavior ka study.Behavior = ethology.
GerontologyAgeing process ka study.Old age study.
MycologyFungi / कवक ka study.Myco = fungi.
NeuroscienceNervous system and brain ka biological study.Neuro = nerves.

🦠 Microbiology Detail

Microorganisms naked eye se usually nahi dikhte. Microscope chahiye. Examples: bacteria, virus, fungi.

🧬 Genetic Engineering Detail

Genes hereditary traits ko carry karte hain. Genetic engineering mein desired traits combine karke new traits banaye jaate hain.

✅ Easy HookBiology mein pehle Chemistry jodi to Biochemistry, Maths joda to Biometrics, Physics joda to Biophysics.
❌ Common MistakeLecture memory hook bacteria-water aur virus-air bolta hai. Actual biology mein exceptions ho sakti hain, isliye exam statement ko carefully read karo.
Mnemonic: Bio ke 3 दोस्त - Chem, Maths, Physics = Biochemistry, Biometrics, Biophysics.
⭐ Most Asked in ExamsGenetic Engineering, Microbiology, Physiology, Ecology aur Father facts high-yield hain.

🧠 Quick Quiz

1. Living beings ke chemical processes ka study?
Biochemistry / जैव रसायन.
2. Organisms and environment ka study?
Bionomics / Ecology context ke hisab se. Bionomics specifically organism-environment relation.
3. Ageing process ka study?
Gerontology.
