1) Science kya hai?

Simple Definition

Science = सुव्यवस्थित / क्रमबद्ध ज्ञान

  • Nature aur physical world ke baare me jo knowledge observation, experiment aur testing se milta hai, use science kehte hain.
  • Science ka Hindi word = विज्ञान.
  • Ye word Latin ke Scientia se aaya mana jata hai.

Exam Line

याद रखने लायक एक लाइन:

“वास्तविक अनुभव, प्रयोग और परीक्षण पर आधारित सुव्यवस्थित क्रमबद्ध ज्ञान को विज्ञान कहते हैं।”

Shortcut: Observe → Experiment → Test → Organized Knowledge = Science

2) Science ki 4 subject matter

चार मुख्य विषय

  • प्रकृति (Nature)
  • ब्रह्मांड (Universe)
  • द्रव्य (Matter)
  • ऊर्जा (Energy)

Matter vs Energy

  • द्रव्य / Matter: jo space gherata hai aur jiska mass hota hai.
  • ऊर्जा / Energy: jiska mass aur volume nahi hota, par effect feel hota hai.
  • Examples of Matter: pen, water, air, stone
  • Examples of Energy: light, heat, sound, electricity

Nature

  • जैविक (Biotic): plants, animals, microorganisms
  • अजैविक (Abiotic): water, air, land, rocks

Universe

  • Stars
  • Planets
  • Satellites
  • Space bodies

3) Science ki main branches

Natural Science

प्राकृतिक विज्ञान

  • Nature aur uske components ka study
  • Isme aage 2 parts yaad rakho:
  • पदार्थ विज्ञान (Material Science)
  • जीव विज्ञान (Life Science / Biology)

Social Science

सामाजिक विज्ञान

  • Human society aur social life ka study
  • Examples: Economics, Political Science, Sociology, Geography
Tree Trick: Science = trunk, Natural Science + Social Science = 2 big branches, phir unke andar chhoti branches.

7) Self test for exam

Q1. Science ka simple meaning kya hai?

Ans: व्यवस्थित / क्रमबद्ध ज्ञान based on observation, experiment and testing.

Q2. Matter aur energy me basic difference?

Ans: Matter has mass and occupies space; energy ka effect hota hai but mass/volume nahi hota.

Q3. Optics kis ka study hai?

Ans: Light.

Q4. Acoustics kis ka study hai?

Ans: Sound waves.

Q5. Metrology kis ka study hai?

Ans: Measurement and units.

Q6. Seismology kis se related hai?

Ans: Earthquake.

Q7. Cryogenics kis par based hai?

Ans: Very low temperature.

Q8. Astronomy aur Astronautics me difference?

Ans: Astronomy = space objects ka study; Astronautics = space travel related study.

8) Last minute revision

  • Science = organized knowledge.
  • 4 subject matter = Nature, Universe, Matter, Energy.
  • Main branches = Natural Science + Social Science.
  • Natural Science = Material Science + Biology.
  • Mechanics-force, Thermodynamics-heat, Optics-light, Acoustics-sound, Electricity-charge, Magnetism-magnet.
  • Atomic Physics-atom, Nuclear Physics-nucleus, Astronomy-space, Seismology-earthquake, Cryogenics-low temperature.
