🌐 Environment: Meaning & Definition

Shabdik Arth

  • परि = charo taraf / all around.
  • आवरण = jo ghere hue ho / covering.
  • Isliye पर्यावरण ka literal sense: jo jeevon ko charo taraf se ghere hue hai.
  • Teacher ne perimeter/circumference se “परि” ko relate karke samjhaya.

Deep Definition

Environment = biotic components aur abiotic components ke beech ka mutual interaction / parasparik sah-sambandh.

  • Biotic = Biosphere / जैव मंडल.
  • Abiotic = Lithosphere, Hydrosphere, Atmosphere.
  • Jeev-jantu, plants, humans air-water-land ke saath interaction karte hain.
EPA 1986 ke according definition

Environment Protection Act 1986 ke context me environment ko physical/biotic conditions aur unki interaction ke roop me samjha gaya. Teacher ne clarify kiya: भौतिक = अजैविक = Physical, aur जैविक = Biotic.

🧠 Environment Ki Characteristics

Key Features

  • Environment biotic aur abiotic elements se milkar bana hai.
  • Environment परिवर्तनशील hai: weather, temperature, rain, wind, clouds change hote rehte hain.
  • Jeev environment ke according अनुकूलन / adaptation dikhate hain.
  • Environment resources ka bhandar hai: water, air, soil, forests, minerals.

Self System

  • स्व नियंत्रण: Environment apne aap ko regulate aur heal kar sakta hai.
  • Corona lockdown example: pollution sources band hue to air and water quality improve hui.
  • स्व पोषण: Plants photosynthesis se food banate hain.
  • Food chain/food web me plants base support dete hain.

Memory Hook: “B-PARS”

Biotic-Abiotic, Parivartansheel, Adaptation, Resource bhandar, Self-control/self-nutrition.

Kyuki weather, temperature, rain, wind, cloud formation jaise factors continuously change hote hain.
स्व पोषण / self-nutrition. Plants apna food photosynthesis se banate hain.

Troposphere / क्षोभ मंडल
  • Earth ke bilkul paas wali layer.
  • Thickness: poles par approx 8 km; equator/vishuvat rekha par approx 18 km.
  • Reason: equator par heat zyada, air light aur expanded; poles par cold dense air compressed.
  • All weather phenomena yahin: rain, wind, cloud formation, lightning, hail.
  • Temperature generally height ke saath decrease karta hai.
Stratosphere / समताप मंडल
  • Approx 18-50 km.
  • Ozone layer yahin hoti hai.
  • Ozone = O₃; oxygen molecule O₂ me ek oxygen aur judta hai.
  • Ozone Sun ki ultraviolet/parabaigani rays ko rokta hai.
  • Ozone depletion se skin diseases, skin cancer, plants damage jaise effects ho sakte hain.
  • Exam me aeroplane ke liye mostly Stratosphere answer mark karna bataya gaya; practical flight upper troposphere/lower stratosphere me hoti hai.
Mesosphere, Ionosphere, Exosphere
  • Mesosphere: 50-80 km; sabse kam temperature.
  • Ionosphere: 80-640 km; ions/charges ki wajah se electrical aur magnetic events; temperature badhta hai; Thermosphere bhi kaha.
  • Exosphere: 640-1000 km; Hydrogen aur Helium gases ki adhikta.

Mnemonic: “क्ष-स-म-आ-ब”

क्षोभ → समताप → मध्य → आयन → बहिर. Neeche se upar isi order me layers revise karo.

🏔️ Abiotic / अजैविक Factors

Abiotic factors = jisme jaan nahi hoti. Inhe physical components / भौतिक संघटक bhi bol sakte hain.

स्थलीय / Terrestrial

  • Lithosphere / स्थल मंडल.
  • Soil / मृदा.
  • Rocks / शैल / चट्टानें.
  • Minerals and ores / खनिज, अयस्क.
  • Landforms: mountains, plateaus, plains, desert.

वायुमंडलीय / Atmospheric

  • Gases ka covering.
  • Water vapour / जलवाष्प.
  • Temperature.
  • Winds / हवाएं.
  • Rainfall and clouds.

जलीय / Hydrological

  • Surface water: नदी, तालाब, झरने.
  • Oceanic water: sea and oceans.
  • Groundwater: underground water.
  • Examples: कुआं, नलकूप, hand pump, boring.

Groundwater Alert

  • Underground water recharge hona zaroori hai.
  • Overuse se big cities me groundwater level niche ja raha hai.
  • Transcript examples: Chennai me groundwater crisis; Bengaluru bhi risk par bataya.

🌱 Biotic / जैविक Factors

Plants / पादप

  • Small plants and big trees sab included.
  • Photosynthesis se apna bhojan banate hain.
  • Food chain ka base support.

Animals / जीव-जंतु

  • All animals included.
  • Human/मनुष्य ko teacher ne “सबसे बड़ा/पढ़ा-लिखा जानवर” style me explain kiya.
  • Humans environment ko heavily modify karte hain.

Microorganisms / सूक्ष्म जीव

  • Virus / विषाणु.
  • Bacteria / जीवाणु.
  • Fungi / कवक.
  • Ye helpful bhi ho sakte hain aur harmful bhi.

Disease Link

  • Virus: air me spread/panapne ka example; Corona virus mention hua.
  • Bacteria: dirty water se bacterial diseases.
  • Fungi: body/sade-gale padarth/achar par phaphund.
  • Communicable diseases, prevention, health indicators future biology topics me cover honge.

🏭 Human-made Environment / मानव निर्मित कारक

Teacher ne bataya ki natural biotic-abiotic ke alawa humans naye environment/ecosystems create karte hain. Isliye human-made environment ko third factor/component ke roop me yaad rakhna hai.

कृषि उत्पादन क्षेत्र

Fields, crops, wheat, rice, bajra, sugarcane, pulses etc. Human-created agricultural environment.

औद्योगिक संकुल

Factories, industries, industrial complexes; alag production environment.

परिवहन संकुल

City transport systems: bus, train, airplane and related networks.

वाणिज्यिक / व्यापारिक संकुल

Trade and commerce zones; markets and business environment.

Mnemonic: “K-O-P-V”

Krishi, Oudyogik, Parivahan, Vanijyik. Human-made environment ke four examples.

Agricultural production area, industrial complex, transport complex, commercial/trade complex.
