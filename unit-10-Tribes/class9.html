👶 Janm, Mrityu aur Social Customs

Birth customs, death memorials aur samajik dंड par direct statement-based question aata hai.

Birth & Youth Customs

  • Dongar Dalwai: Navjat shishu ko makka ke dher par litaana; sir uttar aur pair dakshin ki taraf.
  • Santa Pratha: Saatve din prasuta ko haldi laga kar snan karaya jata hai.
  • Dam Pratha: 12 saal ke ladke ki daahini bhuja par tapta teer daaga jata hai.

Death & Memorial

  • Gata: Mrityu ke baad memorial/shilp banane wali parampara.
  • Matana / Mautana: Udaipur side me prachalit; khoon-kharabe/mrityu par jurmana vasool kar samajik faisla.

Bhangada / Jhagda Pratha

Ye ek social penalty/settlement system hai. Agar shaadi-shuda vyakti kisi dusre ke saath chala jaye ya vivaah kare, to samaj ke log baithkar purane spouse ke liye paisa/compensation decide karte hain. Isse police-court case se bachne wali samajik vyavastha ke roop me samjhaya gaya.
