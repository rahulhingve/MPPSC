🗺️ Gond Ranking, Origin & Location

MP ki second largest tribe aur location-based facts

MCQ 1: MP ki doosri sabse badi Janjati

  • Question: Madhya Pradesh ki दूसरी सबसे बड़ी जनजाति kaun si hai?
  • Answer: Gond Janjati.
  • Teacher sequence: 1st — Bhil, 2nd — Gond, 3rd — Kol.
  • Gond Janjati MP ke सभी जिलों में देखने ko mil sakti hai.
  • Bhil tribe MP ke western हिस्से mein batayi gayi; Bhil ka nivas-sthan “faliya”, ghar “ku/tapra”, bade gaon “pal”.

Kond se Gond — Name origin

  • Question: Gond shabd Telugu bhasha ke kis shabd se nikla? Answer: Kond.
  • Kond/Kon ka अर्थ: पहाड़.
  • Teacher link: Gond log pahadi areas mein rehte hain, isliye name ko Kond/पहाड़ se jodo.
  • Vindhyachal range: Alirajpur side se Chhatarpur tak example diya.
  • South side: Satpura range ka mention hua.
  • Gond ka मुख्य area: Madhya Pradesh ka दक्षिण-पूर्वी area.

Chhindwara + Patangarh facts

  • Question: MP ke kis district mein Gond सबसे ज्यादा? Answer: Chhindwara.
  • Mandla-Dindori side mein bhi Gond बहुलता batayi gayi.
  • Dindori ka Patangarh village Gond painting ke liye famous hai.
  • Patangarh ko “चित्रकारों का गांव” kaha gaya.
  • Gond painting styles/examples: Digna Kala aur No Dora चित्रकारी.
  • Literacy definition teacher ne batayi: 7+ years, normal language ko padh, likh aur samajh sakta ho.

  • Statements A-D mostly सही थे; option E said “सभी कथन गलत हैं”.
  • Teacher logic: E wali बात itself गलत है, इसलिए asked-as-false ke हिसाब से E answer banega.
  • Exam tip: Question stem ko पहले पढ़ो — सत्य/असत्य/एक से अधिक carefully.
