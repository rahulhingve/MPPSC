🎯 Origin & Distribution | उत्पत्ति और क्षेत्र

Bhil tribe ka basic identity, naming, original place aur MP distribution yahan revise karo.

Naam ka Origin

  • भील शब्द ko teacher ne Sanskrit भिल्ल se joda.
  • भिल्ल ka meaning class me धनुष-पाण / धनुषधारी bataya gaya.
  • Ek aur origin: Dravidian word भील se bhi mana gaya.
  • Memory hook: Bhil = Bow, yani धनुष-बाण se identity.

Location Facts

  • मूल स्थान: Rajasthan me Kumbhalgarh ke paas Dhol/Dhol area class me bola gaya.
  • MP me western belt: Alirajpur, Jhabua, Dhar, Ratlam, Mandsaur.
  • Teacher ne Bhil ko MP ke western parts me important bataya.
  • भामट/Bhomat ko Bhil-bahulya area ke roop me revise karaya.

🔥 Exam Hot

  • भील को वन पुत्र Colonel Tod ne kaha.
  • भील को तीरंदाज Ptolemy ne kaha.
  • MP western districts wali line repeatedly important boli gayi.

Quick Recall

Q. Bhil shabd ka Sanskrit source kya bataya?
भिल्ल, meaning धनुष-पाण/धनुषधारी.
Q. वन पुत्र kisne kaha?
Colonel Tod.
Q. MP me Bhil tribe kin districts me boli gayi?
Alirajpur, Jhabua, Dhar, Ratlam, Mandsaur side.

Social Structure

  • झोपड़ी/घर: कू / टापरा.
  • कू/टापरा milkar फाल्या, फाल्या milkar पाल.

  • गमेती ki help: पटेल; Patel ki help: तड़वी.

Roles & Ritual Persons

  • सबसे पवित्र व्यक्ति: भगत.
  • पुजार: औषधियों ki knowledge rakhta hai.
  • कोटवार: चौकीदार type role.
  • बड़वा: तांत्रिक क्रिया karta hai.
  • गायक: बड़वा ki help karta hai; कामड़ी rakhta hai.
  • भोपे ka bhi zikr hua.

Economy & Occupation

  • मुख्य व्यवसाय: कृषि.
  • पहाड़ी खेती: चीमाता / सी-माथा.
  • पहाड़ी area ke Bhil: पालवी.
  • मैदानी area ke Bhil: वागड़ी; plain farming ko class me दजिया/दजिन jaisa bola gaya.
  • पशुपालन, शिकार, forest products bechna.
  • Alirajpur ke Jobat me पंजा दरिया; Neemuch me Nandna block print famous bola.

Food, Dress & Ornaments

  • Food/drink: राबड़ी, महुआ, ताड़ी, कड़कनाथ मुर्गा.
  • Women: श्रृंगार-priya, गुदना/tattoo pasand.
  • जेला: sir par chandi ka ornament.
  • सुंदरी: red sari; पीरिया: yellow dress type, wedding context.
  • अंगरखा, पोत्या, ठेपाना (tang dhoti) revise kiye gaye.
Constitution & Gotra Notes
Article 15(4): socially/educationally backward classes aur SC/ST ke liye special provisions.
Article 46: State ka duty hai SC/ST aur weaker sections ke educational/economic interests promote karna.
Article 350A: linguistic minority children ko primary stage par mother tongue me instruction facility.
Gotra examples: स्यान, निंगवाल, सिंघाड़िया. Same gotra marriage nahi karte.
हल परंपरा: ek-dusre ki help karne wali community-help tradition.

💍 Marriage Customs | विवाह प्रणाली

Bhil marriages, mediator, Dapa system aur wedding rituals.

Core Rules

  • Marriage mediator: भांज गड़िया / बांज गढ़िया.
  • Same gotra me marriage nahi karte.
  • दापा/दपा प्रथा: bride price; वर पक्ष gives to वधू पक्ष.
  • Dapa practice ko reduce/stop karne ke liye 2016 se साथीदार आंदोलन bola gaya.

Dapa Types

  • Cash: normal नगद dapa.
  • खांडा कूटा: maveshi/cattle ke roop me dapa.
  • सावा: anaj/grain ke roop me dapa.
Marriage Types
Vivaah Meaning
भगोरिया विवाह Bhagoria me gulal se choice, family consent ke baad marriage.
गोल गधेड़ो विवाह Festival/dance context me partner choice/run-away style; now often family consent.
नात / नाथरा विवाह Widow remarriage.
घर घुसी विवाह Girl ladke ke ghar me ghus kar rehne lage; forced/insistent union style.

विनिमय विवाह Exchange/atta-satta marriage.

मंगनी विवाह Bhajgadiya mediation, consent aur dapa decision ke saath engagement-marriage.
Wedding Rituals
हल्दी पीठी: boy/girl priests Kajli Mata ke devre jate; girl ko bangles; haldi ritual.

पहरान भी: mama dwara bhanja/bhanji ke liye clothes lana, bhat/mayra jaisa.
लात मार शाला नृत्य: barat aane par bride side se dance context class me bola.

🎪 Festivals & Fairs | पर्व, उत्सव, मेले

Bhagoria, Gal, Garh, Divasa, Indal aur Beneshwar Mela ke direct exam facts.

Bhagoria Festival

  • Major Bhil fair/festival; start Gujarat side se boli gayi.
  • Holi se pehle 7 din tak.
  • 3 parts: Gulaliya 2 din, Gol Gadhedo 3 din, Ujaliya/Ujad 2 din.
  • Pranay/parichay festival type: gulal se partner choice.
  • Jhabua-Alirajpur area me prominent.
  • Gher/Gair dance, mahua/tadi, groups/toliyan ka mention.

Other Festivals

  • गल उत्सव: Gal Dev/Gal Baba ke samman me; Holi ke 2nd day ritual.
  • गढ़ उत्सव: Holi ke 12th/13th day.
  • दिवासा पर्व: Indra/Bav Dev ko prasann karne ke liye.
  • इंदल: Manokamna puri karne ke liye आयोजन.
  • Bhil log Holi, Diwali, Dussehra jaise Hindu festivals bhi manate hain.

Beneshwar Mela

  • Location: Dungarpur, Rajasthan.
  • Sangam: Mahi, Som, Jakham rivers.
  • Called: भीलों का कुंभ.
  • Annual fair: January-February, class me 4 din ka bola.
  • Mahi river Tropic of Cancer ko do baar cut karti hai, class memory line.

🔥 Exam Hot

  • Bhagoria = Holi se pehle 7 din; Gulaliya + Gol Gadhedo + Ujaliya.
  • Gal Utsav = Holi ke dusre din; Garh Utsav = Holi ke 12th/13th din.
  • Beneshwar Mela = Dungarpur, Mahi-Som-Jakham Sangam, Bhilon ka Kumbh.

Quick Recall

Q. Beneshwar Mela kin nadiyon ke sangam par?
Mahi, Som, Jakham.
Q. Bhagoria kitne din?
Holi se pehle 7 din.
Q. Divasa Parv kisliye?
Indra/Bav Dev ko prasann karne ke liye.
