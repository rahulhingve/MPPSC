✅ Article 16(4) — Aarakshan (आरक्षण)

4 → Chaaar → Naav mein baith ke sab ko aarakshan!

Agar rajya ko lage ki pichhde varg ko paryapt pratinidhitv nahi mila → Aarakshan diya jaayega

⬆️ Article 16(4A) — Promotion mein Aarakshan (पदोन्नति)

77th Amendment — 1995

A = Apple khayenge + Promotion milega!

SC/ST ko sarkari naukri mein promotion mein bhi aarakshan milega — agar paryapt pratinidhitv nahi hai to.

🔄 Article 16(4B) — Backlog Pad Carry Forward

81st Amendment — 2000

B = Ball se khelenge → Backlog pad carry forward honge!

Jo reserved seats bhari nahi gayi → wo agley saal carry forward hongi.

50% ki seema mein ye backlog seats count nahi hongi (alag se ginne jaayengi).

Quick Summary:
• 16(4) → Aarakshan milega
• 16(4A) → Promotion mein bhi (77th, 1995)
• 16(4B) → Backlog carry forward (81st, 2000)
⚖️ Indira Sawhney Case 1992 & 50% Limit
इंदिरा साहनी केस — आरक्षण का 50% नियम — LANDMARK CASE

📜 Background Story:

1. Article 340 → Rashtrapati pichhdon ke liye aayog banayega

2. Kaka Kalelkar Aayog (1953) → Recommendations nahi suni gayi

3. Morarji Desai govt → Mandal Aayog (1979) → 3000+ jaatiyan pichhdi → 27% reservation recommend

4. VP Singh govt (1990) → OBC ko 27% reservation de diya

5. PV Narasimha Rao govt → UR mein bhi 10% diya → Supreme Court ne reject kar diya

⚡ Supreme Court ke 2 Key Rulings:

#RulingHindi
1Aarakshan 50% se zyada nahi hogaआरक्षण 50% से अधिक नहीं
2Backlog pad carry forward nahi hongeबैकलॉग पद आगे नहीं बढ़ेंगे

Yaad trick: Indira jee ab nahi SAHEGI (Sawhney) → 1992 → 9-2 = 11 → Remember!

Fir sarkar ne kya kiya?
• 77th Amend. (1995) → 16(4A) joda → Promotion mein aarakshan
• 81st Amend. (2000) → 16(4B) joda → Backlog carry forward + 50% seema mein nahi ginenge

🗳️ Articles 243D & 243T — Panchayat/Nagar Reservation
अनुच्छेद 243D एवं 243T — पंचायत और नगरीय निकाय में आरक्षण

📜 Background:

73rd Amendment (1992) → Bhag 9 joda → Panchayat (Art. 243 to 243-O) → Anusuchi 11

74th Amendment (1992) → Bhag 9A joda → Nagar Nikaay (Art. 243P to 243ZG) → Anusuchi 12

Dono PV Narasimha Rao ki sarkar mein aaye

243D — PANCHAYAT

D = Daas (janajaati log daas the)

243D(1): SC/ST ke liye aarakshan

243D(2): Unki mahilaon ko 1/3 (ek tihai) seats reserved

Gram, Janpad, Zila — sab panchayaton mein

243T — NAGAR NIKAAY

T = Tree (garmi mein ped ke neeche baithe)

243T(1): SC/ST ke liye aarakshan

243T(2): Unki mahilaon ko 1/3 seats reserved

Nagar Parishad, Nagar Palika, Nagar Nigam — sab mein

Quick Formula:
• 243D = Panchayat mein aarakshan | D(1) = ST seats | D(2) = 1/3 mahila
• 243T = Nagar mein aarakshan | T(1) = ST seats | T(2) = 1/3 mahila
• Dono mein 1/3 mahila aarakshan compulsory!
